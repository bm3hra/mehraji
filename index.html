About Vaishali Vatika

Welcome to Vaishali Vatika, an affordable yet premium housing project by Aadarsh Affordable Housing Pvt. Ltd. Located in the rapidly developing area of Sirsi Road, Bhankrota (Vaishali Extension), Jaipur.

Enjoy excellent connectivity to Gandhi Path and Vaishali Nagar. The project is strategically located on an 80 ft sector road (Khasra: 490, 491, 492, 493 & 498/2), ensuring a peaceful lifestyle without compromising on city access. Launched in Nov 2019 and successfully completed in 2026.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We're here to help you understand better.

1. What is the exact location of Vaishali Vatika?

It is located on an 80 ft sector road in Sirsi, Bhankrota (Vaishali Extension), Jaipur, providing excellent connectivity to Gandhi Path and Vaishali Nagar.

2. Are the flats ready to move in?

Yes, the project was successfully completed in 2026 and all flats are 100% Ready to Move-In immediately.

3. Is the project JDA approved and RERA registered?

Absolutely! Vaishali Vatika is a JDA Approved project and is fully registered under RERA (Reg No: RAJ/P/2017/485).

4. Are bank loans available for purchasing a flat?

Yes, easy housing loans are available from almost all leading government and private banks for eligible buyers.

5. What kind of amenities do residents get?

Residents get access to 30+ modern amenities like Gym Area, Temple, Community Hall, Badminton Court, Kids Play Area, and 24x7 CCTV Security.
